Overwhelm leaves clues. The Bandwidth Compass™ helps you notice yours — and see where the strain is really sitting.

This is your first-response diagnostic. Like a paramedic checking your pulse, the Bandwidth Compass™ takes a rapid reading of your four core life systems (Money, Time, Well-being, and Environment) to get the first signs of critical overload. It's the crucial first step to know exactly where the emergency is.

This is not a quiz. There are no scores or grades. The Bandwidth Compass™ is a guided diagnostic designed to help you identify where your life feels most strained — so you know exactly where to begin.

At the end, you will receive a personalized Bandwidth Snapshot™ — a clear, honest summary of what you shared, what it reveals, and one grounded first step.
