Frequently asked questions

What is the Bandwidth Tax™?

It's the hidden cost you pay when strain builds across your four core systems — money, time, environment, and well-being. It shows up as lost focus, depleted energy, and the sense that everything is harder than it should be. Burnout is the symptom; the Bandwidth Tax™ is the cause.

What is From Clues to Capacity™?

It's the guided system for individuals: one journey in three steps — diagnose where capacity is draining, decide what to reallocate, then understand the deeper pattern. It's bought and used as one system, not separate tools.

Do I buy the tools separately?

No. For individuals, From Clues to Capacity™ is one complete system. You move through the steps as a single guided experience.

Is this a quiz, with scores or grades?

No. It's a guided diagnostic — no scores, no grades. The point is to help you see where the strain is and what to do next.

How do organizations get started?

Organizations begin with a short discovery conversation, then a briefing or a 30-day pilot (5–25 employees). Employees get confidential clarity; leadership receives anonymized, aggregate findings only — never individual results.

How is organizational data kept private?

Individual responses stay confidential. Leadership only ever sees aggregated, anonymized patterns, and reports are released only once a minimum of five participants has been met.

Is The Bandwidth Note™ free?

Yes. It's a free weekly note for individuals — one clue, one reflection, and one way to think differently about your bandwidth. Subscribe here.
